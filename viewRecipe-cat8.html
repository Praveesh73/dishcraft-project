Drinks & Smoothies


  1. Ingredients:
    • 1 ripe mango, peeled and diced
    • 1 banana
    • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
    • 1/2 cup coconut milk (or regular milk)
    • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
    • Ice cubes (optional)

    Instructions:
    1. Combine mango, banana, Greek yogurt, coconut milk, and honey in a blender.
    2. Blend until smooth. Add ice if you prefer a colder, thicker smoothie.
    3. Serve chilled with a slice of mango or a mint sprig as garnish.


  2. Ingredients:
    • 1 teaspoon matcha powder
    • 1/2 cup hot water
    • 1/2 cup milk
      (or almond milk for a dairy-free option)
    • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
    • Ice cubes

    Instructions:
    1. Whisk the matcha powder and hot water until dissolved and frothy.
    2. In a separate glass, add ice cubes and pour the matcha mixture over them.
    3. Add milk and honey, stirring to combine.
    4. Serve cold with a straw for a refreshing drink.


  3. Ingredients:
    • 2 cups whole milk (or any milk of your choice)
    • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
    • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar (adjust to taste)
    • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • Pinch of salt
    • Whipped cream or marshmallows for topping
      (optional)

    Instructions:
    1. Heat the Milk:
      • In a small saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat until warm but not boiling. Stir occasionally to prevent it from scalding.
    2. Mix Cocoa and Sugar:
      • In a separate small bowl, whisk together the cocoa powder, sugar, and a pinch of salt.
    3. Combine Ingredients:
      • Add the cocoa mixture to the warm milk and stir until completely dissolved and smooth. This may take a minute or two to ensure there are no lumps.
    4. Add Flavor:
      • Stir in the vanilla extract for added flavor.
    5. Serve:
      • Pour the hot chocolate into a mug.
      • Top with whipped cream or marshmallows, if desired.


  4. Ingredients:
    • 10 fresh mint leaves
    • 1 lime, cut into wedges
    • 1 tablespoon sugar
    • 1/2 cup club soda
    • Ice cubes

    Instructions:
    1. Muddle mint leaves and lime wedges in a glass with the sugar to release the juice and oils.
    2. Fill the glass with ice cubes and pour club soda over it.
    3. Stir gently to combine.
    4. Garnish with a sprig of mint and a lime wedge.
