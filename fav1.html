Spaghetti Bolognese


Ingredients:
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh Parmesan cheese for topping

Instructions:
  1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until browned. Remove excess fat.
  3. Add onion and garlic to the skillet and cook until softened.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Serve the sauce over spaghetti and top with fresh Parmesan.
