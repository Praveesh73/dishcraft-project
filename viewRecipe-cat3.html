Soups & Stews


  1. Ingredients:
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • 1 medium onion, chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 4 cups canned crushed tomatoes
    • 1 cup vegetable broth
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
      (or 2 tablespoons fresh basil)
    • 1/4 cup heavy cream (optional)
    • Salt and pepper to taste

    Instructions:
    1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, cooking until softened (about 5 minutes).
    2. Add crushed tomatoes, vegetable broth, and basil. Stir to combine and bring to a simmer.
    3. Cook for 15-20 minutes, then blend the soup using an immersion blender or a regular blender until smooth.
    4. Stir in heavy cream (optional) and season with salt and pepper.
    5. Serve with fresh basil on top, and enjoy!


  2. Ingredients:
    • 1 lb beef stew meat, cubed
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 3 carrots, peeled and sliced
    • 3 potatoes, peeled and cubed
    • 4 cups beef broth
    • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
    • Salt and pepper to taste

    Instructions:
    1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Brown the beef stew meat in batches, then remove from the pot and set aside.
    2. In the same pot, sauté the onion and garlic until softened (about 5 minutes).
    3. Add tomato paste, beef broth, carrots, potatoes, and thyme. Bring to a simmer.
    4. Add the browned beef back into the pot, cover, and cook for 1.5-2 hours, or until the beef is tender.
    5. Season with salt and pepper before serving.


  3. Ingredients:
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 medium onion, diced
    • 2 carrots, diced
    • 2 celery stalks, diced
    • 3 garlic cloves, minced
    • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
    • 1 teaspoon paprika
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric (optional)
    • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional, for heat)
    • 1 cup dried lentils, rinsed
    • 6 cups vegetable or chicken broth
    • 1 (14.5 oz) can of diced tomatoes
    • 1 bay leaf
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • 2 cups chopped fresh spinach or kale (optional)
    • Juice of 1 lemon (optional, for serving)

    Instructions:
    1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion, carrots, and celery, and sauté until softened (about 5–7 minutes).
    2. Add garlic, cumin, paprika, turmeric, and cayenne pepper. Stir for 1 minute until fragrant.
    3. Add lentils, broth, diced tomatoes, and the bay leaf. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer.
    4. Cook uncovered for 25–30 minutes, or until lentils are tender.
    5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir in spinach or kale if using, and cook for another 5 minutes.
    6. Remove the bay leaf. Serve hot with a squeeze of lemon juice, if desired.


  4. Ingredients:
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 medium onion, diced
    • 2 garlic cloves, minced
    • 1 teaspoon chili powder
    • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
    • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
    • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional, for heat)
    • 6 cups chicken broth
    • 1 (14.5 oz) can of diced tomatoes
    • 1 (4 oz) can of diced green chiles
    • 1 (14.5 oz) can of black beans, rinsed and drained
    • 2 cups cooked, shredded chicken
    • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh, canned, or frozen)
    • Juice of 1 lime
    • Salt and pepper to taste

    For Serving:
    • Tortilla strips or crushed tortilla chips
    • Shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, or a Mexican blend)
    • Diced avocado
    • Chopped cilantro
    • Sour cream

    Instructions:
    1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and cook until softened (about 5 minutes).
    2. Stir in garlic, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, and cayenne pepper. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
    3. Add chicken broth, diced tomatoes, green chiles, black beans, shredded chicken, and corn. Bring to a boil.
    4. Reduce the heat and simmer for 15–20 minutes to let the flavors meld.
    5. Stir in lime juice and season with salt and pepper to taste.
    6. Serve hot, topped with tortilla strips, cheese, avocado, cilantro, and a dollop of sour cream.
