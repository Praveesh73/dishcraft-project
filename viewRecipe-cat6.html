Baked Goods


  1. Ingredients:
    • 2-3 ripe bananas, mashed
    • 1/3 cup melted butter
    • 1 teaspoon baking soda
    • Pinch of salt
    • 3/4 cup sugar
    • 1 large egg, beaten
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

    Instructions:
    1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a loaf pan.
    2. In a mixing bowl, mash the bananas with a fork. Stir in the melted butter.
    3. Add the baking soda, salt, sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract. Mix well.
    4. Stir in the flour until just combined.
    5. Pour the batter into the loaf pan and bake for 60-65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
    6. Let the banana bread cool before slicing and serving.


  2. Ingredients:
    • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
    • 1 cup warm milk
    • 1/2 cup sugar
    • 1/2 cup melted butter
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 2 eggs
    • 1/2 cup brown sugar
    • 1 tablespoon cinnamon

    Instructions:
    1. In a bowl, combine yeast, warm milk, and sugar. Let sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
    2. Add melted butter, salt, and flour, then mix in eggs. Knead the dough for about 8 minutes until smooth.
    3. Cover the dough and let it rise for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
    4. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    5. Roll out the dough into a rectangle, spread melted butter over it, and sprinkle with brown sugar and cinnamon.
    6. Roll the dough into a log and slice it into 12 rolls.
    7. Place rolls in a greased baking dish and let rise for 30 minutes.
    8. Bake for 25 minutes, until golden brown. Serve warm with icing.


  3. Ingredients:
    • 1 cup sourdough starter (active)
    • 1 cup warm water
    • 3 cups bread flour
    • 1 1/2 tsp salt
    • 1 tablespoon honey or sugar
      (optional, for a hint of sweetness)

    Instructions:
    1. Mix Dough: In a large bowl, combine sourdough starter, warm water, and flour. Stir until a dough forms.
    2. Knead: Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead for 10 minutes, or until smooth and elastic.
    3. First Rise: Place the dough in a lightly greased bowl and cover. Let it rise for 4-6 hours, or until doubled in size.
    4. Shape Dough: Punch the dough down and shape it into a round loaf.
    5. Second Rise: Place the dough on a parchment-lined baking sheet and let it rise for another 2 hours.
    6. Bake: Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C). Bake the bread for 30-40 minutes or until golden brown and hollow when tapped on the bottom.
    7. Cool: Allow the bread to cool on a wire rack before slicing.


  4. Ingredients:
    • 1 cup flour (all-purpose or whole wheat)
    • 1/2 tsp baking soda
    • 1/4 tsp salt
    • 1/4 cup coconut oil (melted) or vegan butter
    • 1/4 cup brown sugar
    • 1/4 cup maple syrup or agave nectar
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • 1/2 cup dairy-free chocolate chips
    • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (optional)

    Instructions:
    1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
    2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.
    3. Mix Wet Ingredients: In another bowl, mix coconut oil, brown sugar, maple syrup, and vanilla extract until smooth.
    4. Combine: Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until combined. Stir in chocolate chips and nuts (if using).
    5. Form Cookies: Drop spoonfuls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet.
    6. Bake: Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until edges are golden brown.
    7. Cool: Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack.
