Vegetarian & Vegan


  1. Ingredients:
    • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
    • 2 cups mixed vegetables
      (carrots, cauliflower, bell peppers, peas)
    • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
    • 2 tablespoons curry powder
    • 1 teaspoon turmeric
    • Salt to taste
    • Fresh cilantro for garnish

    Instructions:
    1. Heat coconut oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, and ginger, cooking until softened (5 minutes).
    2. Stir in curry powder, turmeric, and salt, then add the mixed vegetables. Cook for 5 minutes.
    3. Add coconut milk and bring to a simmer. Cook for 20-25 minutes, until vegetables are tender.
    4. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.


  2. Ingredients:
    • 1 block firm tofu, pressed and cubed
    • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
    • 2 cups mixed vegetables
      (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots)
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
    • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
    • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar

    Instructions:
    1. Heat sesame oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Add tofu cubes and cook until golden on all sides. Remove from the pan.
    2. In the same pan, sauté garlic, ginger, and mixed vegetables for 5 minutes.
    3. Stir in soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and rice vinegar, and return tofu to the pan. Stir to combine.
    4. Serve hot with rice or noodles.


  3. Ingredients:
    • 1 cup quinoa (or rice)
    • 1 cup chickpeas (cooked or canned)
    • 1 cup kale or spinach, chopped
    • 1/2 avocado, sliced
    • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
    • 1/2 carrot, julienned
    • 1/2 cup roasted sweet potatoes (diced)
    • 2 tbsp tahini
    • 1 tbsp lemon juice
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • Salt and pepper, to taste
    • Sesame seeds or hemp seeds for garnish (optional)

    Instructions:
    1. Cook Quinoa: Rinse the quinoa and cook it according to package instructions.
    2. Prepare Veggies: Roast sweet potatoes with a bit of olive oil, salt, and pepper at 400°F (200°C) for 20-25 minutes until tender. Slice avocado, cucumber, and carrot.
    3. Make Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together tahini, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper until smooth.
    4. Assemble: In a large bowl, layer the quinoa, chickpeas, kale or spinach, avocado, cucumber, carrot, and roasted sweet potatoes.
    5. Top and Serve: Drizzle with tahini dressing and sprinkle sesame or hemp seeds on top. Serve immediately!


  4. Ingredients:
    • 2 medium sweet potatoes,
      peeled and diced
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tsp smoked paprika
    • 1 tsp cumin
    • 1/2 tsp chili powder
    • Salt and pepper, to taste
    • 8 small corn tortillas
    • 1/2 cup red cabbage, shredded
    • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
    • 1/4 cup lime juice
    • 1/2 avocado, sliced
    • Salsa or hot sauce (optional)

    Instructions:
    1. Roast Sweet Potatoes: Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss diced sweet potatoes with olive oil, smoked paprika, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Roast for 25-30 minutes until tender.
    2. Prepare Toppings: In a bowl, mix shredded cabbage with lime juice. Slice avocado and prepare any additional toppings like cilantro or salsa.
    3. Warm Tortillas: Heat tortillas in a dry pan over medium heat for 1-2 minutes per side.
    4. Assemble Tacos: Fill each tortilla with roasted sweet potatoes, cabbage slaw, avocado, and cilantro. Top with salsa or hot sauce, if desired.
