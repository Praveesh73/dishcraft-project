Appetizer & Snacks


  1. Ingredients:
    • 1 baguette, sliced
    • 2 large tomatoes, finely diced
    • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
    • Salt and pepper to taste

    Instructions:
    1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. Place the baguette slices on a baking sheet and toast them in the oven for 5-7 minutes until golden brown.
    3. In a bowl, combine diced tomatoes, basil, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.
    4. Spoon the tomato mixture onto the toasted baguette slices.
    5. Serve immediately as a fresh appetizer.


  2. Ingredients:
    • 12 large mushroom caps, stems removed
    • 1/2 cup cream cheese, softened
    • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

    Instructions:
    1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
    2. In a bowl, mix the cream cheese, Parmesan, breadcrumbs, garlic, salt, and pepper.
    3. Spoon the mixture into each mushroom cap, pressing lightly to pack.
    4. Arrange the stuffed mushrooms on a baking sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes until golden and bubbly.
    5. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve warm.


  3. Ingredients:
    • For the Guacamole:
      • 3 ripe avocados
      • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
      • 1-2 small tomatoes, diced (remove seeds)
      • 1-2 cloves garlic, minced
      • 1 small lime (juice)
      • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
      • 1/4 teaspoon chili powder (optional)
      • Salt and pepper to taste
      • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped (optional)
      • 1 small jalapeño, finely chopped (optional for spice)
    • For the Tortilla Chips:
      • 1 bag of tortilla chips (store-bought) or homemade tortilla chips:
        • Corn tortillas (cut into triangles)
        • Vegetable oil for frying or baking
        • Salt to taste

      Instructions:
      1. Prepare the Guacamole:
        • Cut the avocados in half, remove the pits, and scoop out the flesh into a mixing bowl.
        • Mash the avocados with a fork or potato masher, leaving it as chunky or smooth as you prefer.
        • Add the finely chopped onion, diced tomatoes, minced garlic, lime juice, ground cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Stir until combined.
        • If using cilantro, jalapeño, or other optional ingredients, mix them in.
        • Taste and adjust seasoning, adding more salt, lime juice, or spice if desired.
      2. Prepare the Tortilla Chips (if making homemade):
        • Fried: Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan or deep fryer over medium-high heat. Fry the tortilla triangle pieces until golden and crispy. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels. Sprinkle with salt while still hot.
        • Baked:Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Cut the corn tortillas into wedges, drizzle with a little oil, and sprinkle with salt. Arrange in a single layer on a baking sheet and bake for 10-15 minutes, or until golden and crispy, flipping halfway through.
      3. Serve the freshly made guacamole with a bowl of tortilla chips on the side.


  4. Ingredients:
    • 1 pint cherry or grape tomatoes
    • 1 pack of fresh mozzarella balls
      (bocconcini or ciliegine)
    • Fresh basil leaves (whole)
    • Balsamic glaze or balsamic vinegar reduction
      (for drizzling)
    • Olive oil (for drizzling)
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Wooden skewers or toothpicks

    Instructions:
    1. Assemble the Skewers:
      • Take a skewer or toothpick and begin threading the ingredients in this order: 1 cherry tomato, 1 fresh basil leaf, 1 mozzarella ball, and another cherry tomato. Repeat this order for each skewer.
    2. Drizzle with Olive Oil:
      • Arrange the assembled skewers on a platter and lightly drizzle with olive oil.
    3. Add Balsamic Glaze:
      • Drizzle balsamic glaze (or a balsamic vinegar reduction) over the skewers for a sweet and tangy finish.
    4. Season:
      • Sprinkle the skewers with salt and pepper to taste.
    5. Serve:
      • Serve immediately or refrigerate briefly until ready to serve.
